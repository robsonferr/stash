How AI credits work

Each AI credit equals 1 AI action inside Stash. Free can keep using a personal API key, while Pro and Premium include monthly credits out of the box.

Reminder inclusion 1 credit covers 1 reminder inclusion. Available in Free, Pro, and Premium.

FAQ

What usually blocks the buying decision?

Which plan should I choose first?

Choose Pro if your biggest pain is speed: finding tasks, getting basic analysis, and adding a paid layer without much complexity. Choose Premium if you want the fuller system with organization, insights, collaboration, and sync.

How fast do I get access?

Checkout is handled by Stripe. After payment, your subscription state syncs through the licensing service so you can activate inside the Stash app using the same email you used to buy.

Can I change plans or billing later?

Yes. You can start monthly, switch later, or manage billing after activation. Yearly is priced at 10x monthly, which gives you 2 months free.
